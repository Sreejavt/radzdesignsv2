Website Optimization

Fixing key issues of existing sites

Who is this for?

We help organisations whose websites:

  • Break across tablets and mobile devices.
  • Take too long to load on mobile or desktop.
  • Have content that shifts, jumps, or displays incorrectly.
  • Have messaging issues and fail to communicate to the right audience.
  • Perform poorly after website builder (e.g., WordPress) updates.
  • Don't show up on Google despite a good website structure.

Why it Matters?

These issues affect you in the long run because:

  • Visitors struggle to use your website on different devices.
  • Slow pages discourage users from exploring further.
  • Layout issues make your business appear less professional.
  • Unclear messaging makes it harder for visitors to understand what you offer.
  • Website updates can introduce new problems that affect the user experience.
  • Poor Google visibility makes it harder for potential customers to discover your business.

Website Redesign

Reworking the design, structure and content of existing sites

Who is this for?

We help organisations whose websites:

  • Are outdated and no longer reflect their business.
  • No longer match their current branding, products, or services.
  • Have a confusing page structure that's difficult for visitors to understand.
  • Have calls-to-action that are scattered, inconsistent, or missing.
  • Use visuals that don't support or reinforce the message.
  • Have grown over time without a clear structure or direction.
  • Need a complete redesign rather than isolated improvements.

Why it Matters?

These issues affect your organisation in the long run because:

  • Your website no longer reflects who you are as an organisation.
  • Visitors struggle to understand your products, services, or value proposition.
  • An outdated website can reduce trust and credibility.
  • Poor website structure makes it harder for visitors to complete important actions.
  • Inconsistent branding weakens your organisation's identity.
  • Your website becomes harder to maintain and scale as your organisation grows.
  • Business opportunities can be lost because your website no longer supports your current goals.
