Our Vision

To be a leading global innovative SaaS organisation that partners with businesses, VCs, and academic institutions to deliver high-quality solutions that could help organizations to scale and grow.

Our Mission

To deliver customized high-quality business solutions to companies to decrease costs in customer acquisition and employee retention. We support SaaS/Fintech/Product Companies through mapping workflows, identifying challenging areas and designing systems to reduce the churn and drop-offs.

Our Story

Radz Designs is an organization focused on providing specialized services in the domains of customer experience (CX) and employee experience (EX). Founded in 2026, the organization strives to be a trusted partner for SaaS, Fintech, D2C, and product development companies, delivering cutting-edge solutions to help with cost cutting, scaling, and growth.

Radz developed as the brainchild of SJ, who noticed that most companies in the market are more interested in developing features than in letting customers know about them and discover the value. As a result, most features go unnoticed in the market, contrary to companies' expectations that they are meeting customer needs.

The main objective of Radz is to reduce this mismatch and establish a long-lasting connection between the companies and the customers. This will help to reduce the acquisition and retention costs, thus reducing the inefficiencies and attaining operational excellence in digital domains.

Meet the Founder

Sreeja (SJ)

Founder, Radz Designs

Sreeja (SJ) is a Customer Experience (CX) and Employee Experience (EX) enthusiast and strategist, and the founder of Radz Designs.

Her interest in these areas began when she started her career in journalism and later opted for an MSc in Applied Psychology, where her project was to study the correlation between job involvement and job satisfaction. While working with people across different walks of life, she also developed a well-rounded understanding of customer/user needs and their experiences with different products.

These experiences helped her understand the realities faced by marketing, growth, and HR teams and shaped her approach to building simple, value-centric systems and solutions.

Through Radz, she collaborates with business teams across the digital space to develop customized, proprietary, easy-to-implement frameworks to bring clarity, improve workflows, and deliver operational excellence.

Radz investigates and maps existing systems, identifies operational challenges, and proposes frameworks to optimize the system.
